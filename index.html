Tyrrell County is a diverse economic and ecological destination located between the northeast North Carolina urban mainland and the Outer Banks. Founded in 1729 and named for one of the Lords Proprietors, it is North Carolina’s least populous county and boasts beautiful nature, outdoor recreation, and a welcoming community.
