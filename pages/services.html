Personnel Policy Manual

The personnel policy establishes a system of personnel administration for Tyrrell County that provides a program to recruit, select, employ, develop and maintain an effective and responsible work force. This policy also covers condition of employment, employee benefits, work rules, grievance procedures, position classification and pay administrator.
