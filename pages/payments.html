Tyrrell County offers a wide variety of convenient payment options for our customers. Simply choose the option that best suits your needs.

Simply choose the option that best suits your needs!

Pay Your Bill Online - Save some time!

Tyrrell County is pleased to offer our customers the convenience of online bill payment. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express. Simply click one of the links below and you will be taken to our secure bill payment portal. On MyGovHub you can make a one-time payment by clicking “Try QuickPay” or you can create a profile which will allow you to access, view, and pay your bills online 24/7. There is a convenience fee associated with paying your bill by credit/debit card of 3.5% per transaction. Please note that Tyrrell County Utilities does not receive the money for this convenience fee that is collected by the credit card processing company.

Payment By Phone

Customers can call to make their payments over the phone. Find the corresponding number for the type of payment below, along with the matching convenience fee attached. Please note that Tyrrell County Utilities does not receive the money for this convenience fee that is collected by the credit card processing company. Payments by e-check will include an additional $2.00 service fee

  • Water/Sewer: 1-252-796-137 - (3.5% per transaction)
  • Taxes/Fees: 1-252-796-2675 - (2.95% per transaction)
  • Inspections/Permits: 1-252-796-4128 - (2.95% per transaction)
Payment By Mail

If paying by check or money order through the mail, please send all payments with your payment coupon. To avoid late fees, please mail your payment at least five to seven business days before the due date specified on your bill.

  • Address: Tyrrell County Utilities PO Box 449 Columbia, NC, 27925
Payment at our Office Location

You may pay your bill in person at our office address. We accept cash, check, money orders, Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express. There is a convenience fee associated with paying your bill by credit/debit card of 3.5% per transaction for water/sewer and 2.95% for taxes and permits . Please note that Tyrrell County Utilities does not receive the money for this convenience fee that is collected by the credit card processing company. Payments by e-check will include an additional $2.00 service fee

  • Address: 108 S. Water St. Columbia, NC 27925
